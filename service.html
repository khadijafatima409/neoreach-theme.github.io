Our Process

STEP 1

Develop a data-
backed strategy

Beginning with your goals, our team
reverse engineers a launch strategy
derived from a myriad of data
including competitor audits, audience
demographics matching, & channel
-specific opportunities.

STEP 2

Source top-
performing niche
influencers

Our in-house tools help us find the
perfect influencers for your campaign.
Large or small, fashion or fishing,
selecting creators with an intentful fan
base using a world-class profile
analysis technology.

STEP 3

Coordinate
communication &
logistics

With the right influencers selected, our
team will manage the entire process
including introduction, contract
negotiation, deployment, shipping,
payments, and a maintained
relationship for future activations.

STEP 4

Track campaign
performance &
share insights

Results are everything. Our dedicated
dashboards allow both your team and
ours to understand which influencers,
social channels, types of content, and
audiences performed the best.
